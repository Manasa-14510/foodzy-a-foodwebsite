About Foodzy

Delivering happiness, one meal at a time

Our Story

Foodzy was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to connect people with their favorite foods in the most convenient way possible. What started as a small operation with just a handful of restaurant partners has grown into a platform that serves thousands of customers daily.

Our journey began when our founders, a group of food enthusiasts, realized that there was a gap in the market for a food delivery service that prioritized quality, speed, and customer satisfaction above all else. They set out to create a platform that would not only make food delivery seamless but also support local restaurants and provide job opportunities in the community.

Today, Foodzy is proud to be a leading food delivery service, connecting customers with a diverse range of cuisines from the best restaurants in town. We continue to innovate and improve our services, always keeping our customers' needs at the heart of everything we do.
